## Example 1: AI Automation — "Leads Miss Ho Rahe Hain?"

## Example 2: Web Design — "Website Se Trust Kaise Bane?"

### Scene 1 (HOOK — Style C):
```
3D caricature illustration, vertical 9:16 (1080x1920px). Young Indian male 
caricature (enlarged head, dark hair, goatee, questioning raised eyebrow, 
slight smirk) wearing a purple hoodie with hood down. Pose: arms crossed, 
head tilted, looking skeptically at a laptop showing a poorly designed 
old website.

Bright home office with warm lighting — wooden desk, coffee mug with steam, 
colorful poster on wall, small plant. The laptop screen shows a cluttered, 
outdated website design (bright enough to see).

Bold text: 'Tumhari Website Se Log Bhaag Rahe Hain? 🏃'. 
Floating stat cards:
  '75% logo se judge karte hain' (blue card)
  '53% 3 sec me chale jaate hain' (orange card)

Hand-drawn question marks around character, sad face doodle near the laptop, 
small running figure sketch.

Handle '@ravi.digital.solutions' bottom-left.
Mood: relatable curiosity, slightly humorous.
Colors: purple, cream, warm orange accents, wood brown.
```

### Scene 3 (TRANSITION — Style C):
```
3D caricature illustration, vertical 9:16 (1080x1920px). Same young Indian 
male caricature (knowing smile, confident wink) wearing a purple hoodie now 
with a smart blazer over it. Pose: standing in center, left hand pointing 
left, right hand pointing right — comparison gesture.

Split composition feel — left side shows a faded/desaturated old ugly 
website mockup on a dim screen. Right side shows a vibrant, colorful 
modern website mockup on a bright glowing screen.

Left card: '❌ Purana Design' (red-tinted, faded)
Right card: '✅ Modern Design' (green-tinted, vivid)
Arrow sweeping from left to right showing transformation.

Lightbulb doodle above character's head (idea moment), stars and sparkles 
on the right side, dim/crossed elements on left.

Handle bottom-left. Mood: revelation, turning point.
Colors: transition from dull warm → vibrant purple/blue/green.
```

## Example 4: Motivational — "Meri AI Journey"

### Scene 1 (HOOK — Style A, rare emotional use):
```

(1080x1920px). A young Indian male character (short dark hair, light beard, 
determined expression) wearing a simple white t-shirt and jeans, sitting 
alone at a desk late at night with a laptop open. The room is warmly lit

sunrise starting to appear.

visual storytelling and emotion.

Colors: warm amber, soft orange, cream, touches of blue from laptop screen.
Mood: quiet determination, inspiring, nostalgic.

NOTE: Style-A usage — this is for rare emotional/journey content only.
```
