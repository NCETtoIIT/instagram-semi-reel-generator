❌ Audience Pain Points
  • Website dikhne mein achhi hai, par leads ya sales generate nahi ho rahi.
  • Website load hone mein bohot time lagati hai, khaaskar mobile devices par.
  • Visitors ko website par next kya action lena hai, ye clear nahi hota (no clear Call To Action).
  • Website mobile-friendly nahi hai ya mobile par acha experience nahi deti, jisse potential clients chale jaate hain.
  • Website ka content business ke baare mein zyada batata hai, customers ki problems solve karne ke baare mein kam.
  • Website par trust build karne ke liye social proof (testimonials, reviews) ki kami hai.
  • Navigation confusing hai, users ko jo information chahiye wo jaldi nahi mil paati.
  • Website par high bounce rate hai, matlab visitors aate hain aur turant nikal jaate hain.
