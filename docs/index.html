🔍 Complete Research Insights
📊 Statistical Proof
  • India ka e-commerce market 2026 tak $200 billion aur 2029 tak $326.7 billion tak pahunchne wala hai.
  • India mein 78% D2C traffic mobile se aata hai aur 75% e-commerce sales smartphones se hoti hain.
  • Mobile websites ka average conversion rate sirf 1.5% hai, jabki mobile apps ka 4.5% tak hota hai – yani apps se 3x zyada conversions.
  • Overall e-commerce conversion rates 2.5% se 3% ke aas-paas rehte hain.
  • Ek second ki page load delay se conversions mein 7% ki kami aa sakti hai.
  • MSMEs (Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises) jinhone e-commerce apnaya, unka market reach 50% badha aur revenue mein 30% ka boost mila 2 saal mein.
  • 20% se zyada small businesses abhi bhi online sales channels use nahi karte.
  • Globally 70% se zyada carts abandon ho jaate hain.
❌ Audience Pain Points
  • Online sales kam ho rahi hain aur cart abandonment rate bohot high hai.
  • Website slow load hoti hai, especially mobile par, jisse customers wapas chale jaate hain.
  • Online payments aur security ko lekar customers ka trust nahi ban pa raha, Cash on Delivery (COD) par zyada depend karte hain.
  • Competition bohot zyada hai, aur online alag dikhna mushkil ho raha hai.
  • Logistics aur deliveries mein delays aur high costs se customer dissatisfaction badhti hai.
  • Product search aur product ki information theek se na hone se customers ko decision lene mein problem hoti hai.
  • Website par customers ke experience ko personalize karna mushkil lagta hai.
