❌ Audience Pain Points
  • Outdated website design credibility kam karta hai aur business ko untrustworthy dikhata hai.
  • Website sirf ek digital brochure jaisi hai, leads generate karne ke liye optimized nahi hai.
  • Visitors ko pata nahi chalta ki next step kya hai, kyunki website par koi clear 'Call-to-Action' (CTA) nahi hai.
  • Slow loading speed ke karan high bounce rates aur frustrated users.
  • Poor mobile optimization ki wajah se website phones par theek se nahi chalti, jo majority traffic ko frustrate karta hai.
  • Confusing navigation aur poor user experience (UX) ke karan visitors website chhod dete hain.
  • Website par trust signals (testimonials, reviews) ki kami hone se credibility nahi banti.

  • Website ka content audience se connect nahi karta ya value proposition clear nahi hai.
  • Business owners ko nahi pata ki unki website se kitni leads aa rahi hain ya kya kaam kar raha hai (no tracking/analytics).
